About Me

I am a MSc student majoring in Geomatic Engineering at ETH Zurich (opens new window). Previously, I obtained my B.S. in Geographical Information Science from Wuhan University (opens new window). I also interned at the University of Alberta (opens new window) and DeepMap (opens new window). My research interests lie in Geoinformatics, Geospatial Computer Vision and Remote Sensing.
