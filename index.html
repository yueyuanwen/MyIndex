About Me

I am a MSc student at ETH Zurich and conducted my master thesis at the ETH AI Center. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Wuhan University. I also interned at the University of Alberta and DeepMap. My research interests lie in 3D computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly enthusiastic about digitizing of real-world environments and semantic scene understanding.
